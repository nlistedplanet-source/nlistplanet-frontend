Favicon Setup Instructions

To create favicon from your logo:

Method 1: Online Favicon Generator

  1. Go to: https://favicon.io/favicon-converter/
  2. Upload your logo.png file
  3. Download the generated favicon files
  4. Place favicon.ico in /public/ folder

Method 2: Simple Conversion

  1. Resize your logo to 32x32px
  2. Save as favicon.ico
  3. Place in /public/ folder

Current Setup

Favicon will be automatically loaded from /public/favicon.ico
